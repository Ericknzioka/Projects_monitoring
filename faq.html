Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Projects Progress Monitoring System?

It is a secure web-based system for students and supervisor to share research projects progress and also communicate on the changes through the system not neccessary meeting physically

2. How do I get access to the system?

You must register as a student or supervisor officer, then wait for admin approval.

3. How secure are my Projects?

All projects files are secure as you need to share the files with specific supervisor whom you select and the system offers strong authentication mechanism for to be able to access.

4. Can I download my projects?

Yes, after successful uploads or receiving them from supervisor.

5. Who can I contact if I encounter issues?

You can use the internal messaging system to contact the supervisor directly from your dashboard.
